About the Author

Rian Mistry is a literature enthusiast, aspiring educator, and critical thinker with a passion for exploring the intersections between literature, history, and global socio-political affairs. Through The Thinking Quill, Rian aims to create a space where deep reading and intellectual reflection meet accessible, engaging essay writing.

With an academic background rooted in English literature and a curiosity for how texts shape and reflect cultural consciousness, Rian writes with an emphasis on historical context, philosophical underpinnings, and the human condition. Whether analyzing modernist fiction, colonial discourse, or contemporary media, each piece is crafted to ignite meaningful thought.

Rian is currently pursuing higher education and preparing for a career in teaching and research. This blog serves not only as a creative outlet but also as a platform to connect with like-minded readers who value nuance, critical thinking, and literary exploration.

“To think deeply is to read between the lines.”
